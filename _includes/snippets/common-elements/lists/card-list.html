Biodiversity

Global Forest Watch Biodiversity, an application under development through a partnership with RESOLVE, will unite the best available data for forests and biodiversity to monitor and manage critical habitats and species. With new biodiversity indicators, monitoring capabilities, and the longer-term build-out of interactive dashboards, GFW Biodiversity will supply the information needed to assess and help countries reach international binding biodiversity commitments.
